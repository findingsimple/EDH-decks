## Notes
**Strategy:** The Ur-Dragon's eminence ability reduces all Dragon spells by {1} even from the command zone, letting you deploy threats ahead of curve. When Dragons attack, you draw cards and can cheat permanents directly onto the battlefield. Ramp early, then overwhelm with undercosted Dragons.

**Key Synergies:**
- Eminence + Dragonspeaker Shaman + Dragonlord's Servant + Urza's Incubator = Dragons cost 4+ less
- Attack triggers + Miirym, Sentinel Wyrm = draw cards, make token copies of non-legendary Dragons
- Tiamat tutors five Dragons on ETB, filling your hand for future turns
- Savage Ventmaw generates mana on attack to cast more spells mid-combat
- Scion of the Ur-Dragon tutors any Dragon into the graveyard (reanimation target)
- Lathliss, Dragon Queen creates 5/5 Dragon tokens whenever non-token Dragons enter
- Utvara Hellkite creates 6/6 Dragon tokens for each attacking Dragon

**Win Conditions:**
- Massive flying Dragon board swinging for lethal
- Utvara Hellkite / Lathliss token avalanche
- Atarka, World Render giving all Dragons double strike
- Cruel Ultimatum for card advantage and opponent disruption
- Mana Echoes generating huge mana from Dragon tribal synergy
