## Notes
**Strategy:** This deck combines Elf tribal synergies with -1/-1 counter/proliferate themes. Morcant triggers blight on every Elf ETB, so the goal is to flood the board with Elves (via token generators like Lathril, Wolverine Riders, Elvish Promenade, Lys Alana Huntmaster) and then proliferate those counters to kill enemy creatures.

**Key Synergies:**
- Morcant + mass Elf token generation = mass blight triggers
- Proliferate effects (Evolution Sage, Karn's Bastion) grow -1/-1 counters
- Crumbling Ashes destroys creatures with -1/-1 counters each upkeep
- Chronicle of Victory gives all Elves +2/+2, first strike, and trample
- Guardian Project / Beast Whisperer for card draw
- Witherbloom, the Balancer casts for drastically reduced cost with a wide Elf board (affinity for creatures) — a 5/5 BG Elder Dragon finisher

**Win Conditions:**
- Overwhelm with pumped Elves (Coat of Arms, Ezuri, Chronicle of Victory)
- Shaman of the Pack / Exsanguinate for direct life loss
- Lathril's activated ability (tap 10 Elves to drain 10 life)
- Triumph of the Hordes for an infect overrun — lethal with a wide counter-stacked Elf board
- Lock down enemy boards with proliferated -1/-1 counters

**Tutor Targets:**
- **Demonic Tutor** (any card to hand) priorities:
  - **Triumph of the Hordes** — infect overrun for a 10-poison kill on a wide board.
  - **Coat of Arms** — tribal anthem that scales with Elf count, often lethal immediately.
  - **Doubling Season** — doubles token generation and counter placement, massively accelerates blight.
  - **Witherbloom, the Balancer** — discounted by affinity for creatures, comes down as a 5/5 finisher.
  - **Priest of Titania** / **Marwyn, the Nurturer** — if mana-starved, these turn a wide board into absurd ramp.
  - **Cradle-style ramp** if available (Gilt-Leaf Palace, Yavimaya).
- **Elvish Harbinger** (ETB: tutor an Elf to top of library) priorities:
  - **Priest of Titania** for Elf-count mana ramp.
  - **Ezuri, Renegade Leader** for trample + regeneration + pump.
  - **Lys Alana Huntmaster** to chain Elf tokens off every subsequent Elf cast.
  - **Beast Whisperer** for card draw on every creature cast.
- **Green Sun's Zenith** (X mana, green creature to battlefield) priorities:
  - X=1: **Llanowar Elves** / **Elvish Mystic** / **Fyndhorn Elves** — turn-1 ramp dork.
  - X=2: **Priest of Titania** — Elf-count mana.
  - X=3: **Marwyn, the Nurturer** / **Elvish Archdruid** — ramp + growth.
  - X=5: **Ezuri, Renegade Leader** — finisher/pump piece (or **Witherbloom, the Balancer** if the board is wide enough to cast him naturally).

**Upgrade Ideas:**
- Collected Company (more creature tutoring)
- Overwhelming Stampede (backup finisher)
- Growing Rites of Itlimoc (ramp)
